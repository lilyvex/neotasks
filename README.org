** Contributing
Contributions are always welcome, regardless of skill level, but there are a few basic
requirements to ensure that they get merged.

- Ensure that pull-requests (PRs) have a managable scope
   - Formatting PRs are exempt from this
- Use [[https://www.conventionalcommits.org/en/v1.0.0/#summary][conventional commits]]
   - For more complex changes, provide a brief description within your commit
- Be available to respond to questions or concerns about your changes
   - And be willing to make changes to your PR
- GPG sign your commits (optional)
- Follow the [[https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/3/0/code_of_conduct/][code of conduct]]
- Ensure that all commits can at least successfully load the Plasmoid

*** Steps to contribute
1. [[https://github.com/lilyvex/neotasks/fork][Fork]] this repository
1. Create a new branch
1. Make and test your changes
1. Create a [[https://github.com/lilyvex/neotasks/compare][pull-request]]
1. Wait for possible feedback or merge
1. If any feedback is provided, discuss changes and then make them
1. Repeat the previous two steps until your changes are merged
